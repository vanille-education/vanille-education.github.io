About Vanille Education

Vanille Education is an independent, open-access platform designed to collect, organize, and showcase the best free online courses and certifications available worldwide.

The project was created by Vanille, combining technology, community, and accessible learning into a single hub. The goal is simple: to make high-quality education reachable for everyone, without barriers.

Our Mission

  • Provide curated lists of free courses from trusted providers.
  • Promote accessible, self-paced learning.
  • Help students, professionals, and creators develop real skills for real careers.

Why This Project Exists

Many platforms hide free certifications, make them hard to find, or bombard you with ads. Vanille Education solves that with a clean, simple, and transparent directory that stays updated and community-driven.

Whether you're improving your CV, learning tech, or exploring new fields, Vanille Education is built to support your journey.
